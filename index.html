Sanket Goutam

Ph.D. Candidate @[Ethos Lab](https://ethos-lab.github.io/) | Security Researcher @[Samsung Research America](https://sra.samsung.com/)

I am a PhD student in the CS department at Stony Brook University, where I am a member of the Ethos Lab and am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Amir Rahmati. My research is broadly focused on security and privacy of cyber-physical systems, IoT ecosystems, and distributed networking systems. Before moving to New York, I was a Software Engineer working on a distributed shared memory infrastructure at HPE Aruba Networks in California.

I received my MS from North Carolina State University, where I was a graduate research student in Wolfpack Security \& Privacy Lab, advised by Prof. William Enck and Prof. Brad Reaves.
